Frequently Asked Questions

Camp FAQ

What is the age range for the camps?

Our Junior Adventure Camp is for ages 8-12, Teen Explorer Camp is for ages 13-17, and the Family Weekend Retreat is open to all ages. Specialty Skills Camp is for ages 10-16.

What should campers bring?

Campers should bring comfortable clothing, sturdy shoes, a water bottle, sunscreen, and any personal items needed for the duration of the camp. A detailed packing list will be provided upon registration.

Are meals provided?

Yes, all camp programs include meals, which are prepared on-site and accommodate dietary restrictions if notified in advance.

What is the cancellation policy?

The $100 deposit is non-refundable. Cancellations made at least 30 days before the camp start date are eligible for a full refund of any additional fees paid. Cancellations within 30 days may receive a partial refund, depending on the circumstances.

Is financial assistance available?

Yes, we offer financial assistance for qualifying families. Please contact our office for more details and to apply.

If you have additional questions, feel free to contact us at info@yourcamp.com.
