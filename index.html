About Our Camp

Our History

Whomp Wagon was born in 2016 when a group of friends, united by their love for heavy bass music and the Burning Man ethos, decided to create a unique camp experience. Starting as a small crew with a single sound system, we’ve grown into a vibrant community known for our high-energy DJ sets, immersive art installations, and welcoming vibe. Each year, we bring our signature “Whomp” energy to the playa, blending deep bass, funky beats, and a commitment to radical inclusion. Our camp has hosted legendary sunrise sets, spontaneous dance parties, and countless connections that last beyond the dust.

Our Mission

We exist to bring together creative humans who defy the norm, support each other, and build unforgettable moments at Kiwiburn.
