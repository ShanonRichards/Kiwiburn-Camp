We exist to bring together creative humans who defy the norm, support each other, and build unforgettable moments at Kiwiburn.

Our History

The Early Days

Cosmic Rejex began as Cosmic Creatures and Camp Riddikulus before adopting its name in 2025, honoring former member Bede Max. It started with a small group of Kiwiburn regulars and newcomers brought together by a love for alternative music and self-expression. With just a few tents, a speaker, and a shared vision, we set out to build something bigger.

The Sound Camp Era

By 2024, we had grown into a full-fledged sound camp with our roots in an emo night collaboration with R.A.T Camp. In 2025, we took over hosting, expanding into DJ sets featuring drum and bass and punk rock. Our team grew from 13 to 35, adding alien-themed art, a chill zone, and more, making Cosmic Rejex a true Kiwiburn fixture.
