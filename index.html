About Our Camp

Join the Rejex... Cosmic Rejex is a welcoming home for emos, rockers, and bass-heads—a safe, inclusive space to connect, unwind, and be yourself. We’re all about good tunes, strange art, deep chats, and the occasional wild conspiracy theory. As a sound camp, you can expect emo anthems, raw rock, and rolling drum and bass, all wrapped in a laid-back, friendly atmosphere. Whether you’re here to dance, chill, or just vibe out, you’re always welcome.

Our History

The Early Days

Cosmic Rejex started as a small group of Kiwiburn regulars in 2020, united by a love for alternative music and self-expression. We set up our first camp with just a few tents, a portable speaker, and a dream to create a space for misfits.

The Sound Camp Era

By 2022, we evolved into a full-fledged sound camp, bringing emo anthems and drum and bass to the playa. Our crew grew, and we introduced alien-themed art installations, making Cosmic Rejex a Kiwiburn staple.

The Future

Now, Cosmic Rejex is looking ahead to expand our inclusive vision. We aim to create a platform where musicians and artists of all genres can share their talents, continuing to grow our community at Kiwiburn and beyond.

Our Mission

We exist to bring together creative humans who defy the norm, support each other, and build unforgettable moments at Kiwiburn.
